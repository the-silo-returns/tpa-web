our goals

  1. To be truly student-led: to have space to explore our own interests and share them with others, to present what we care about to the rest of the school community and to communicate effectively with staff about matters of inclusion.
  2. To create a guide for staff which is inclusive of all students in front of them; to ensure a consistency of approach which keeps students safe andâ€‚allows them to be heard and respected.
  3. To reach out to members of the school community who may not already be aware of the TPA. To raise our profile in school.

where to find us

we gather on thursday lunctime in room 37 (the second floor of the maths block). It's open to all queer people and allies, no matter of age, and there's no obligation to come every week. we are also looking for sixth formers to help run the club. you will not be outed to anyone outside the club.

how to contact us
